ABOUT SIP

A matcha café created with intention.

SIP was created to bring the art of matcha into a space that feels calm, beautiful, and memorable.

Our matcha is sourced from Japan and prepared in small batches with a focus on color, texture, and taste.

Every drink is designed to feel elevated — from the first pour to the final sip.
